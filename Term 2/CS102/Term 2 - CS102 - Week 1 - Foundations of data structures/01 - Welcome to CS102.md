# Welcome to CS102: Data Structures & Algorithms

## 1. Course Overview
Welcome to Computer Science 102. This is one of the most important courses for your AI engineering path: **Data Structures & Algorithms**. 

## 2. Course Structure
The course is organized into weekly units, each focusing on a major theme:
- Organizing data efficiently
- Searching and sorting techniques  
- Algorithm design and analysis
- Advanced structures for real-world software and AI applications

## 3. Learning Progression
- **Foundation**: Basic building blocks of data structures
- **Organization**: Smarter ways to manage collections of data
- **Advanced**: Complex structures and algorithms for real-world applications

## 4. Assessment Structure
### Grading Breakdown
- **Weekly Reviews**: 20% each × 3 weeks = 60% total
- **Course Review**: 40% (comprehensive final review)

### Bonus Opportunities
- **+10 points**: Submit and pass all reviews on time
- **+10 points**: Participate in weekly discussions on time

## 5. Review System
- Each week ends with a unit review to reinforce learning
- Final unit includes comprehensive course review covering all CS102 content
