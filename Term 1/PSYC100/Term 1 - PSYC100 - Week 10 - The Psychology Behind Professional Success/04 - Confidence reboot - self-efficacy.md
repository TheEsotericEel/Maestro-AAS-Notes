# Confidence Reboot: Self-Efficacy

> "Whether you think you can, or you think you can't—you're right." — Henry Ford

## 1. Self-Efficacy (Albert Bandura)
### What is it?
**Self-Efficacy** is not "Self-Esteem" (feeling good about yourself). It is the specific belief in your capability to execute a *specific* task.
-   **Equation**: `Belief in Capability` x `Effort` = `Performance`.
-   **The Glitch**: If you believe you "just aren't a math person," your brain conserves energy and you stop trying. You fail, confirming the belief. (Self-Fulfilling Prophecy).

## 2. The 4 Sources of Confidence
Bandura identified 4 knobs to turn up confidence.

### 1. Mastery Experiences (The "Hello World")
-   **Mechanism**: Small wins release dopamine and rewire the brain to expect success.
-   **Protocol**: Don't try to build Netflix on day 1. Build a Button. Then a Form. Then a Page.
-   **Engineering**: This is why "Tutorials" work. They force a small win.

### 2. Vicarious Experience (The Role Model)
-   **Mechanism**: "If they can do it, I can do it."
-   **Protocol**: Watch others code (Live coding streams, Pair Programming).
-   **Trap**: Watching a genius can hurt efficacy if they seem "magical." Watch someone *struggling and succeeding*.

### 3. Verbal Persuasion (The Coach)
-   **Mechanism**: External validation overrides internal doubt.
-   **Engineering**: A Senior Dev saying "Nice implementation" is worth 10 hours of debugging.
-   **Trap**: False praise ("Good job" on bad code) backfires.

### 4. Physiological State (The Nerves)
-   **Mechanism**: Interpreting body signals.
-   **State**: High Heart Rate + Sweating.
-   **Weak Frame**: "I am panicking. I will fail."
-   **Strong Frame**: "I am excited. My body is preparing for a challenge."

## 3. Engineering Application: Breaking the "Imposter" Loop
When you feel like a fraud:
1.  **Stop Comparing**: Their highlight reel vs. your internal blooper reel.
2.  **Look at the Git Log**: Review your past commits. Proof of previous mastery.
3.  **Just One Line**: meaningful progress on *one* small function reboots the loop.

## ⚡ Key Takeaway
> **Confidence is a memory of success.**
> You cannot "think" your way into confidence. You must "act" your way into it. 
> Write the code. The feeling follows the action.
