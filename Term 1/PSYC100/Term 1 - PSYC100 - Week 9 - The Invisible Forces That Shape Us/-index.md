# Week 9: The Invisible Forces That Shape Us

## 🎯 Overview
This module explores **Social Psychology**—the scientific study of how people's thoughts, feelings, and behaviors are influenced by the actual, imagined, or implied presence of others. For Engineers, this is the study of **Users in Context**.
