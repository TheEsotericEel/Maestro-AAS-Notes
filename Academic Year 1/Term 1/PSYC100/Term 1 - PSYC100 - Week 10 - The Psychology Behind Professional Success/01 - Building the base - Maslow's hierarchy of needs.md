# Building the Base: Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs

> "You cannot run high-performance code on a server with no power."

## 1. The Human Stack
### What is it?
Abraham Maslow (1943) theorized that human needs are arranged in a hierarchy. You must satisfy the lower levels before you can focus on the higher ones.
-   **The Insight**: Motivation bugs are often Dependency errors. You can't debug "Why am I not creative?" (Level 5) if you are "Sleep deprived" (Level 1).

### The 5 Layers (Engineering Edition)
1.  **Physiological (Hardware)**: Sleep, Food, Water, Exercise.
    -   *Dev translation*: Electricity, Cooling, CPU Cycles.
2.  **Safety (Security)**: Job security, financial stability, physical safety.
    -   *Dev translation*: No "Layoff" rumors, Psychological Safety.
3.  **Belonging (Network)**: Friends, Team, Love.
    -   *Dev translation*: Code Reviews, Mentorship, "My team has my back."
4.  **Esteem (Permissions)**: Respect, Status, Recognition.
    -   *Dev translation*: "I am a Senior Engineer," "My PRs are good."
5.  **Self-Actualization (Application)**: Creativity, Problem Solving, Morality.
    -   *Dev translation*: "I am building the future. I am in Flow."

## 2. Debugging Your Life
### The "Halt" Protocol
If you are feeling stuck, anxious, or stupid, run a diagnostic on the stack from the bottom up. **HALT**:
-   **H**ungry? (Layer 1).
-   **A**ngry? (Layer 2/3).
-   **L**onely? (Layer 3).
-   **T**ired? (Layer 1).

### The Cognitive Cost
Unmet needs run as **background processes** that consume RAM (Working Memory).
-   **Scenario**: You are worried about rent (Safety).
-   **Effect**: Your brain reserves 30% of CPU for "Rent Anxiety."
-   **Result**: You become "stupider" at coding because you are running on 70% capacity.

## 3. Engineering Application: Team Health
### For Leaders
-   **Don't buy ping pong tables (Layer 3/5)** if you are paying under market rate (Layer 2).
-   **Don't ask for "Innovation" (Layer 5)** if the team is working 80-hour weeks (Layer 1).
-   **Fix the dependencies first.**

## 4. Practical Protocol: The Maintenance Window
You schedule downtime for servers. Schedule it for yourself.
-   **Sleep**: It is not "time off." It is "Garbage Collection" for the brain (Glial cells clean out toxins).
-   **Food**: Glucose is the fuel for willpower. Low blood sugar = Low discipline.

## ⚡ Key Takeaway
> **Maslow before Bloom.**
> You cannot learn (Bloom's Taxonomy) if you do not exist (Maslow's Hierarchy).
> Treat your biology with the same respect you treat your production database.
