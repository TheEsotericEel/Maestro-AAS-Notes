# Welcome to OOP

## 1. Course Overview

PY103 is about learning to **think in objects**, not just write code. The course is structured in 4 units:

| Unit | Theme |
|------|-------|
| **Unit 1** | Thinking in objects — what OOP is and how objects bundle data + behavior |
| **Unit 2** | Bringing objects to life — creating and using many objects from one blueprint |
| **Unit 3** | When objects meet — how objects relate and work together |
| **Unit 4** | From simple objects to flexible systems — designing cleaner, more flexible programs |
